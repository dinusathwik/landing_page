what is anime

anime is a japanese term for hand-drawn or computer animation. the word is the abbreviated pronunciation of "animation" in japanese, where this term references all animation. outside japan, anime is used to refer specifically to animation from japan or as a japanese-disseminated animation style often characterized by colorful graphics, vibrant characters and fantastical themes. culturally, anime is considered a form of art.
